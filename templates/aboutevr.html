Services

I am a Qualified Registered clinical officer, Holding Diploma in clinical Medicine, Degree in Public Health and currently undertaking a Master’s Degree at Maseno University.

I have experience of 15 years in clinical practice, having worked for Ministry of Health Kenya for 3 years and KEMRI - HIV research program, FACES for 12 years I and My co- Director had a vision to support the disabled clients after he called me to review a chronic ill disabled client who had a myriad of challenged ranging from Socio-economic to Health. This challenges pointed at a neglected population that we thought it is very prudent to start and organization that could lobby, arbitrate and stand the Disabled, and that is how Konya Disability support CBO was hatched. My roles in the Konya Disability support CBO include;

  • Provide leadership and articulate policies for the organization
  • Provide technical advisory on clinical issues
  • Guide and Lead in Research activities
  • Help the program make informative decision
  • Coordinate issues pertaining the running of the program and be a role model to the Organization.
  • Administrative and Financial.
  • Preside over all program meetings.
  • Have the authority to hire staff and terminate in consultation with the board
  • Liaisons duties pertaining the running of the organization locally and internationally
