About Us

About Konya-SPEWID

Konya Disability Support organization was started after doing a research and found how people with disability are neglected and get limited services in the society due to their conditions. Our plan is to advocate and lobby for people with disability in Kisumu East Sub-County, Konya Sub location and to ensure of quality service offered, in cooperating other governors in developing, innovative and flexible opportunities within the community to support individual choice.

There are many factors that affect people living with disabilities. The study concentrated on how health access, socioeconomic challenges and Disability aids ( ramps, lifts, parking-bays, toilets, and brail )affect the service offered to the Blind, the Deaf, cognitive and the physically handicapped.

The study was conducted by literature review indicating that prevalence of disability in Kenya is 10% however there were no published data indicating Disability prevalence within Kisumu County among others. Hence finding disparity in social economic empowerment as true representative of the people living with disabilities (PLWDs.)

We have done our research and are confident that such an organization would be of great support and beneficial to community.

Our Mission

Cultivates an accessible and inclusive community where people with disability have an equal opportunity to participate in all aspects of life.

Our Vision

Provide, Coordinate and support programs that enable people with disability to access services and participate in all aspects of life and to be judged by their ability not disability.
