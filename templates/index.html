Konya Disability Support organization was started after doing a research and found how people with disability are neglected and get limited services in the society due to their conditions. Our plan is to advocate and lobby for people with disability in Kisumu East Sub-County, Konya Sub location and to ensure of quality service offered, in cooperating other governors in developing, innovative and flexible opportunities within the community to support individual choice.
